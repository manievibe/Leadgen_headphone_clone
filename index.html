Headphones are a hardware device that can be plugged into
a computer, laptop, smartphone, mp3 player or other device
to privately listen to audio without disturbing anyone in the
vicinity.


They are electroacoustic transducers, which convert an electrical signal to a corresponding
sound. Headphones let a single user listen to an audio source privately, in contrast to a
loudspeaker, which emits sound into the open air for anyone nearby to hear. Headphones
are also known as earphones or, colloquially, cans..
